Negative Effects Of The G.R.E.

Negative Actions by the G.R.E.

  1. In the middle of the game, the G.R.E. stops sending aid to the city of Harran, which allows many survivors to become fully infected.
  2. When the G.R.E. learns of Dr. Zere's research on the virus, they order the player to stop helping the survivors, and do anything to gain the trust and retrieve the research.
  3. Finally, the G.R.E., relating to reason one and two, plans on not making a cure, but weaponizing the virus using Zere's research, and then selling the virus to the highest bidder.
