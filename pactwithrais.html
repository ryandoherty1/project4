The Pact With Rais

The Pact with Rais, is a quest in the main game shortly after the G.R.E. stops supplying Harran with Antizin drops.

Due to the G.R.E.'s descision, Crane is asked by Brecken to buy Antizin from Rais, the ex-operative of the G.R.E., who defected and stole a file from the G.R.E..

In the end, Rais betrays Crane in multiple ways. Rais makes a deal between the G.R.E., that if he gives them Zere's research, they will get him safely out of Harran, with no consequence.
