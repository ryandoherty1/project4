Benefits Caused By The G.R.E

Positive Benefits of the G.R.E.

  1. The G.R.E periodically provides humanitarian support to the infected city of Harran by C-130 Hercules cargo planes. Packages include food, water, weapons, medical supplies (Antizin, bandages, etc).
  2. At the start of the game, the G.R.E. does care for the lives of survivors. They allow the player to help the survivors, while continuing his main mission.
  3. The G.R.E. has already created a map of the virus to help progress towards a cure for it.
