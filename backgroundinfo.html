About Dying Light

"Dying Light" is a game created by the company, Techland. The game takes place in the Fictional city of Harran. You play as a man named Kyle Crane, a member of the Global Relief Effort "G.R.E.". After a mutation of rabies causes a flesh-eating virus epidemic, and an ex-G.R.E. member goes rogue due to the death of his brother, in which he steals a valuable file to get revenge on the G.R.E., you are sent in to recover the file, and scout the situation of Harran itself.

The Global Relief Effort "The G.R.E"

Kyle Crane is the main character, and the only playable character, in the game "Dying Light". He is an operative of the G.R.E. sent into the city of Harran to recover a stolen file taken by an ex-G.R.E. operative, Kadir, Suleiman.

Kadir Suleiman, aka "Rais", is the first main, human, antagonist you meet in Dying Light. He is the leader of one of the main three factions in Dying Light, and is your first known nemesis.

The Global Relief Effort, aka the G.R.E., is a government company, charged with the control over the Harran epidemic, and providing supplies via air-drops, to the survivors of Harran.
